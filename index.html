Supports Underprivileged in our Society

Established by Shri. P.B. Kulkarni in 1997

Suparn Charitable Trust is a non-government organization (NGO) that supports the underprivileged in our society and provides them with a better platform.
With an aim to educate the poor students and cater the financial needs, Shri. P.B.Kulkarni, former Executive Director of Reserve Bank of India, and Chairman & Managing Director of Bank of Maharashtra, established Suparn Charitable Trust in 1997.

Our Objectives

We believe that providing financial assistance to the deprived will improve their quality of life and help them grab better opportunities too.

Process of Granting Financial Assistance:

The ability of the Trust to render assistance has increased significantly since its inception.
In 1998-99, 7 students were provided financial assistance, while in 2015-16, the number was 92.

The broad criteria followed for grant of scholarships are as under:

  1. The parent's/guardian's annual income should not exceed Rs. 1,00,000 (in 1997 this limit was Rs. 36000).
  2. Assistance is provided for higher education after successful completion of Standard 10(S.S.C).
  3. Marks secured at Standard 10 level is an important factor in deciding whether or not to grant assistance.
    • In order to renew assistance each year, the student will have to secure a minimum of 60% marks.
    • Financial assistance is provided to students across various fields of education, preference being given to courses that are likely to increase the chances of securing a job soon after completion of education, or those that would enable the student to start an independent business.
  4. Special circumstances like extreme poverty, special skills, disability etc. are given due consideration.
